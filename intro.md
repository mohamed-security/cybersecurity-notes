# Introduction to Cybersecurity

## What is Cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber attacks.

## Types of Threats
- Malware
- Phishing
- Brute force attacks
- Social engineering

## Why Cybersecurity is Important
- Protects personal data
- Prevents financial loss
- Keeps systems secure

## What I Learned Today
- Cybersecurity is about both attacking and defending systems
- There are many types of threats
- This field requires continuous learning
